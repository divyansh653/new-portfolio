About Me

Hello! My name is Divyansh Vijay Mate, and I come from the beautiful town of Bhandara. I completed my schooling at Z. P. Nutan Vidyalaya, Mundhari (BK), and pursued my 12th grade at Z. P. High and Junior College, Kardi. I am proud to have achieved 90.40% in my 10th class and 80.50% in my 12th class.

Driven by a passion for learning, I appeared for the Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (MHCET) and the Joint Entrance Exam (JEE). I was thrilled to score 92.77% in MHCET and 90.39% in JEE. Currently, I am pursuing my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering at Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering, Shegaon.
